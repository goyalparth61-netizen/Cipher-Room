# 🎮 About Cipher Room

Cipher Room is not just another escape room game.

It is an AI-powered cybersecurity adventure where every login creates a completely different mission.

Unlike traditional puzzle games, Cipher Room uses Artificial Intelligence to generate:

- New Story
- New Mission
- New Passwords
- New Clues
- New Emails
- New Logs
- New Evidence
- New Ending

No two playthroughs are exactly the same.

---

# 🧠 Story

Year 2042.

The world's largest cyber defense organization has been breached.

An unknown hacker group known only as **Ghost Cipher** has infiltrated multiple classified systems.

You are the newest cyber intelligence agent.

Your AI companion **ARES** is your only guide.

Every mission could save thousands of lives.

Or expose humanity's biggest secret.

Can you escape before it's too late?
